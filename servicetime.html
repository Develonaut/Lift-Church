Early Service

This service will appeal to those looking for a more traditional experience. Each week, we will enjoy singing hymns along with our choir and orchestra. Our pastor will be the primary communicator and the service will last about an hour.

Late Service

10:45AM

Late Service

This service will appeal to those looking for a more contemporary experience. Each week, we will enjoy singing popular worship songs along with the church band. Our pastor and other guests will help deliver the message and the service will last about an hour.

Childrens Service

10:45AM

Childrens Service

This service is meant for children of all ages. This service takes place at the same time as our Late Service and lasts about an hour.
