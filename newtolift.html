JUST FOR YOU

Venice, Florida is an absolutely beautiful area on the Gulf of Mexico. From the beaches, to the parks, to historic downtown—this is a great place to live and raise a family. Rhonda and I are so thankful that God has brought us here and we feel strongly that God will use us to make an impact in Venice and in the many surrounding cities (Nokomis, Englewood, Sarasota, North Port, Port Charlotte, and Osprey) of Southwest Florida.

What's To Explore

Very exciting things are happening here. We truly believe God can change your life. Please take time to stop by one of our church services to meet our ministry staff and the wonderful people that attend Lift Church. You can also view one of our services live over the internet at either 9:00 or 10:45am on Sundays, or 7:00pm on Wednesdays. We’re located at 695 Center Road, right between the Venice YMCA and Pelican Pointe Golf & Country Club; and we’re across from Venice Garden Elementary School.
